Get to know The Lamp Genie

The Lamp Genie was created for those who enjoy the uniqueness of handmade art. It started off as a hobby however, I quickly discoverd that it could be more than just that, it could be a lifeline. I have sold lamps to many different cultures all over the world.


The Lamp Genie has quickly turned into a family business, there is something for everyone to do. I create the lamps and do all the physical work, my son labels them so we know which lamp belongs to who, my daughter does all of the technical work such as email correspondence and processing online orders and my wife does the package and posting.
